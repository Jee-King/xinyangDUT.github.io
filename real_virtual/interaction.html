Real-Virtual Consistent Traffic Flow Interaction

Traffic simulation has become an efficient tool, with the assistance of computer visualizing techniques, to solve traffic issues such as traffic congestion, network design, and similar problems. Properly controlling simulated traffic flow and modeling each vehicle’s irregular behaviors are key issues in the traffic simulation field. In this project, we introduce real vehicle trajectories as a data-driven factor in simulated traffic situations to drive behaviors of other simulated vehicles.
